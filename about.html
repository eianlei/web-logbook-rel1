web-logbook application for viewing DivingLog data

This web application shows my digital scuba diving logbook that is created and maintained with the “Diving Log 6.0” or for short DL6 application.

User Interface documentation

top menu

  • Logbook: Shows the list of all dive logs in a table
  • Dive: Shows the log of a single dive
  • Map: Shows a map of dive site locations
  • Photos: Shows media gallery (photos and videos) from the selected dive
  • Trips: Shows the list of trips in the database.
  • Places: Shows the list of Places (divesites) in the database.
  • Buddies: Shows the list of Buddies
  • Equipment: Shows the list of Equipment
  • Brevets: Shows the list of Brevets (certification cards)
  • ABOUT: this page

Logbook

Shows the list of all dive logs in a scrollable table. One log on each row. - click the a row of a dive to view more details of the dive - by clicking any of the column headers you can sort the table by that header. Clicking the header another time changes the sorting direction.

Dive

Shows the log of a single dive. - On top you will see the profile graph containing: - depth - tank pressure - temperature - by moving mouse over the profile, you can see numeric values at any given point in time - Navigation buttons: Previous, Next, Last dive, dive number - Linked information buttons: Buddy, Trip, Place, Map, Photos linked to this dive - All details - Comments - If there are any URLs in the comment text, then those are converted to hyperlinks, and you can click them to view on new browser tabs

Map

Shows a map of dive site locations. - you can zoom in or out using +/- buttons or the mouse scroll wheel - click on marker to see details of the site

Photos

Shows a media gallery (photos and videos) from the selected dive.

Trips

Shows the list of trips in the database. Clicking a trip row shows details of that trip on panel at right hand side List of dives linked to this trip are shown. You can further click the dive row to see the log of that dive.

Places

Shows the list of “places” (dive sites) in the database. Clicking a Place (dive site) row shows details of that Place on panel at right hand side List of dives linked to this place are shown. You can further click the dive row to see the log of that dive.

Buddies

Shows the list of buddies in the database. Clicking a buddy row shows details of that buddy on panel at right hand side List of dives linked to this buddy are shown. You can further click the dive row to see the log of that dive.

Equipment

Shows the list of equipment in the database. Clicking an equipment row shows details on a panel at right hand side

Brevets

Shows the list of Brevets (certification cards). Clicking a Brevet row shows details on a panel at right hand side
